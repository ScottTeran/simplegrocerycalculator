A Simple Grocery Calculator

About the App

If you're looking for an easy way to keep a tab of how much you’re spending at the grocery store (or anywhere else), look no further! A Simple Grocery Calculator is a handy little app that supports all kinds of Android devices.

With A Simple Grocery Calculator you can quickly add (or remove) multiple values to help keep you within your budget. Just enter the price of the item, press the plus button, and it will be added to the running total. If you need to remove an item, just press the remove icon and the item will be deducted from the total. That’s it. Simple!
